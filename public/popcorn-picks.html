Year

2025

Role

Full-stack developer

Client

Personal Project

URL

www.popcorn-picks.com

Weilding the power of Django, HTMX for interactive UI, and the styling flexibility of Tailwind, Popcorn-Picks was bown.

For the longest time, I have been keeping track of my favorite movies on a Google Sheet with a Google Form attached to it, with some scripting and an extension to autosort by overall average rating. But I wanted an upgrade. In researching what would be the best method/framework to use for the project, Django became a pretty clear option.

I developed:

Fully functional user registration, login and dashboard, allowing users to make their own reviews of films on the site.

Implemented TailwindCSS light and dark mode for best User Experience

HTMX enabled live-search bar, with auto-complete. If you select a movie from this list, it is added to the database as a movie that can be reviewed.
